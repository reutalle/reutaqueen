About Us

Our project is a smart irrigation system that refers to the measurement of soil moisture in several places and performs irrigation operations according to the result of the soil moisture sample. The role of the system is to improve irrigation efficiency, save water and prevent unnecessary waste of resources. The principle of operation of the system is automatic watering as soon as the moisture level in the soil has dropped beyond the predetermined value for the system. The importance of the IOT in this project is expressed in the fact that it allows us to receive information in real time and to activate the system automatically and remotely based on the information received and the pre-defined parameters.
